ABOUT JONATHAN CLARK

Mind & Body Efficiency Expert

Jonathan Clark, commonly referred to as the Mind & Body Efficiency Expert, is recognized as one of the premier Health & Fitness resources in the country. Known for his unique ability to blend Personal Growth, Health & Wealth Creation Strategies with bio-energetic disciplines, his clients consistently achieve results that are faster, better and are truly BUILT TO LAST!

Jonathan has helped countless others to break free of their preconceived limitations and has helped his clients achieve amazing transformations for their body’s, their families, and for their careers!

He received two Bachelors of Science in Economics & History from the University of Virginia, in addition to a Masters in Real Estate Development and Finance from Georgetown University

Prior to becoming a Coach, Jonathan was involved in industries spanning Finance, Real Estate, Construction & Transportation. He had to experience massive pain, in order to experience massive transformation. Being willing to Speak, Preach, and Teach across the country, he has worked with a wide variety of clients, both corporations and individuals alike.

Jonathan is also certified by the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM) and is trained in Six Human Needs Psychology.

Professional Certified Life Coach

As a Professional Certified Life Coach, his experience has allowed him to be personally mentored by the best, and trained through Robbins-Madanes as a Strategic Interventionist, as well as through the International Professional Excellence in Coaching (IPEC) association
