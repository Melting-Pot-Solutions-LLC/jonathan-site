ABOUT JONATHAN CLARK

Mind&Body Efficiency Expert

Jonathan Clark, commonly referred to as the Mind & Body Efficiency Expert, is recognized as one of the premier Health & Fitness resources in the country. Known for his unique ability to blend Personal Growth, Health & Wealth Creation Strategies with bio-energetic disciplines, his clients consistently achieve results that are faster, better and are truly BUILT TO LAST!

As a Professional Certified Life Coach, his experience has allowed him to be personally mentored by the best, and trained through Robbins-Madanes as a Strategic Interventionist, as well as through the International Professional Excellence in Coaching (IPEC) association.

My WHY

I want my life to stand as an excellent example of rebirth, rejuvenation, reinvention and redemption. Because my life was almost taken away, today I see it as a gift. And I want to give away as much of myself as possible, changing lives for the better and changing the world in the process.

My MISSION

To help men and women realize and achieve their highest potential in every aspect of their life from Finance, to Health and Fitness, to Business, to Relationships.

My PURPOSE

To make the world a better place by positively impact the lives of 1 billion people that will have lived a life of success, fulfillment and achievement because of the sole fact that I have existed.

As a leader in this world, I aim to impact a multitude of lives across the globe through incredible determination and my relentless commitment to constantly improving the lives of other people worldwide.
